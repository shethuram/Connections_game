Game Rules

  • The game consists of linking pictures to find a movie name out of it. There are 10 such questions framed.
  • Link the three pictures given for each question and find the name of the Indian movie.
  • Enter the original spelling of the movie correctly. Incorrect spellings will make the answer incorrect.
  • You will be rewarded one point for each correct answer, and the leaderboard will be updated accordingly.
  • You can try as many times as you want if the answer is incorrect; there are no limitations.
  • After successful registration, you can log in anytime and answer the questions within the given time limit. There are no restrictions to attempt all questions at once.
  • The top point scorer will be claimed as the winner. In case of a tie, all those who secured the top score will be awarded as winners.
  • Make sure to enjoy the game and give your best!
